About Me

Hi'ya! I'm Marta and eversince i was a little girl i knew two things: i want to do art and i want to teach people. Now i am a 20yo artist and an art teacher from Latvia. I've been doing art for as long as i remember. From painting and drawing to video and sound design i truly do belive i can do it all if i try hard enough. But there is more to me than just art and teaching stuff. Here's a few things that i like:

  • Climbing trees
  • Dancing
  • Fluffy animals
  • Churches
  • Wine
  • Traveling

I believe that art is not just a reflection of reality or ones state of mide, but a way to escape, make light of and even laugh about it. I realy enjoy switching up the media i work in. I really enjoy video editing colored pencil drawing and sound design. Each medium allows me to express different facets of my creativity and connect with others in unique ways. Through my art and teaching, I hope to inspire others to see the beauty in versatility and the power of expressing oneself through different artistic avenues.
